With a background in programming and a creative mind brimming with new ideas I am looking to merge the gap between developers and designers. When the internet initially went public, it was foreign and hard to navigate because UX/UI was neglected; if this had not been the case, the web could have been accessible so much faster. This is why I am so passionate about creating universally intuitive and thought-provoking interfaces.
